Concept Development





Despite initially looking at 3d scanning we collectively decided against using scanning as one of our medias. After going to the induction, Natasha reported that photogrammetery would be most useful for our project but unfortunately, we couldnt find a use for it that worked with what we already had envisioned for our concept.

After talking to our tutor about the social media research we conducted, we realised we had no way to visualise our findings in a way that was impactful for our project. It was suggested to us that we could gather the comments and input them into an AI engine to create an image as a visual representation of peoples opinions.
