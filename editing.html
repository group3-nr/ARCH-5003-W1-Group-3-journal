Editing



What was the process of editing like and what new skills did you learn?

Amy: It took a while to learn. Trial and error. If we got stuck simply using yt tutorials or googling the problem helped. I feel I am more capable than I was before editing.

I learnt how to do speed ramps, how to apply effects and what effects can be applied, I learnt that premier has a lot of built in transitions that can be used and I liked messing around with that, I also learnt how to make something track a certain object in a moving video.

Gabby: Editing was an interesting thing to learn but ultimately gave me a new skill to use potentially again. 

Editing and researching were new skills that I definitely had to try and get to grips with.

What programs did you use and was there anything you tried that didnt work?

Amy: We used premiere pro. We decided to cut the video a bit short due to it repeating techniques and points so we could conclude our original points.

Gabby: I used iMovie at first and then premiere pro. We had the idea of different transitions at the start with zooming into one another and then ended up not having that because it didn’t fit into the scheme as seamlessly as we would have wanted.

As part of this group project we were constantly giving eachother advice, ideas and feedback. This helped us form a project that we all had input on and some basic knowledge of eachothers areas of focus.
