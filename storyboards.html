Storyboards



Storyboarding is an integral part of the video development process. At every part of the process we were continuously updating and chaniging storyboards to fit our new ideas. When we were more sure of our concept, Caitlin and Natasha were tasked with creating some final storyboards for the group to discuss and refer back to when developing the video. This was to make sure we were all on the same page going forward.





These storyboards each have their own focus for story telling including how to intergrate AI into the video, topical routes and comparison of perceptions. It was decided that we would start with a montage of news clips as that part had already been done when trying out softwares for editing.
